**Directory Fuzzing**

`XeroCyb3r@htb[/htb]$ ffuf -w <file path>:FUZZ -u http://SERVER_IP:PORT/FUZZ`

`XeroCyb3r@htb[/htb]$ ffuf -w /opt/useful/SecLists/Discovery/Web-Content/directory-list-2.3-small.txt:FUZZ -u http://94.237.62.195:41956/FUZZ`
